My Scientific & Open Science Values

Transparency is the bedrock of reproducible science. I advocate for clear workflows — from study design and data acquisition to analytical pipelines and final publication — enabling genuine scrutiny and reuse.

— Jarian Permana

🔓 Open by Default

All analytical code, derived data, and methodological documentation are shared under permissive licenses (MIT / CC-BY). Exceptions only for sensitive species locations.

📐 Robust Methods

Selecting the most suitable statistical & ecological approach with transparent uncertainty quantification and validation, respecting both power and limitations.

🤝 Collaborative & FAIR

Active contribution to open-source projects, peer-review of methods, and welcoming issues/pull requests on GitHub to strengthen conservation data science.
